Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how MovieFlakes handles your data.

🔒

No User Accounts

MovieFlakes does not require registration or user accounts. You can browse the application without providing personal information.

💾

Local Storage

Your watchlist is stored locally in your browser using Local Storage. This information never leaves your device.

🌐

Third-Party API

MovieFlakes retrieves movie information from The Movie Database (TMDB) API. No personal information is shared with TMDB.

🍪

Cookies

MovieFlakes does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

🛡️

Security

No sensitive information is collected or stored. All browsing activity remains on your device.

📅

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as MovieFlakes evolves. Changes will always be reflected on this page.
